# Values and beliefs

## Values

### general conduct
*   **always follow my daily habits** – [identity-based habits](https://jamesclear.com/identity-based-habits) are the foundation of who you are.
*   **never waste a single second of time** – in the worst case, do something for [just a minute towards your goal.](https://vishnubharathi.codes/blog/no-more-zero-days/). 
<br>as seneca wrote, [life is long if you know how to use it](https://dailystoic.com/on-the-shortness-of-life-seneca/).
*   **be energetic every time**
*   **learn something (new) everyday.**
*   **family come first followed by health** – [hierarchy of essentials](https://www.simplypsychology.org/maslow.html).
*   **learn to forgive, mostly** – [the art of forgiveness](https://www.reddit.com/r/AskOldPeople/comments/17ewgz7/the_art_of_forgiveness/). 
    > “holding on to anger is like drinking poison and expecting the other person to die.” – buddha
*   **take the hard road, mostly**
*   **experience things(good/bad) as fast as you can** – [google x projects built on failures](https://www.mediapost.com/publications/article/245866/google-x-projects-built-on-failures.html). 
    > "if you want to increase your success rate, double your failure rate." – thomas j. watson
*   [**never complain, never explain**](https://www.artofmanliness.com/character/behavior/never-complain-never-explain/).

### mindset & thinking
*   **zero regrets, always be grateful** – practice [amor fati](https://dailystoic.com/amor-fati/) (love of fate).
*   **you will be happy not thinking of being it** – the [backwards law](https://markmanson.net/the-backwards-law).
*   **less drama (social media, politics)** – amazing book by cal newport: [digital minimalism](https://ia801802.us.archive.org/22/items/digital-minimalism-by-cal-newport/Digital%20Minimalism%20by%20Cal%20Newport%20.pdf).
*   **if you don't know, say you don't know** – [the power of intellectual humility](https://www.templeton.org/discoveries/intellectual-humility).
*   **first principle to everything** – [reasoning from first principles](https://fs.blog/first-principles/) rather than analogy.
*   **if something takes 2 min, do it** – the [gtd rule](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Getting_Things_Done) by david allen.
*   **if you wanted to procrastinate, just do the work for 2 min** – james clear’s [2-minute starter](https://jamesclear.com/how-to-stop-procrastinating).
*   **if you want to be einstein, do what einstein did at your age** – [modeling excellence](https://nlpco.com/nlp-modeling-excellence/) (nlp strategy).
*   **send the message, meet the person** – use the [regret minimization framework](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jwG_qR6XmDQ).
*   **never live in illusion** – always test against reality (the [reality principle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reality_principle)).
*   **only obsess over things you have power to change** – the [dichotomy of control](https://philosophybreak.com/articles/dichotomy-of-control-a-stoic-device-for-a-tranquil-mind/).



## Beliefs

*   **life is not easy** – [10 bitter lessons i learned from 27 years of life](https://www.reddit.com/r/Life/comments/1l7fu84/10_bitter_lessons_i_learned_from_27_years_of/).
*   **there's no perfectly [right or wrong](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/moral-particularism/), same for people.**
*   **desires can be best and worst at the same time** – the [hedonic treadmill](https://anthonyvicino.com/hedonic-treadmill/) of wanting more.
*   **you don't learn without making mistakes.** – [the value of failing forward](https://www.chrismwilson.com/blog-posts/failing-forward-why-failure-is-necessary-in-life).
    > “insanity is doing the same thing over and over and expecting different results.” – albert einstein
*   **don't multitask** – [multitasking is a myth](https://jamesclear.com/multitasking-myth).
